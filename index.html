Top Three Activities to Do in Islamabad

Visit Faisal Mosque

The Faisal Mosque is the world's fifth-largest mosque and a prominent landmark in Islamabad.

Visit Pakistan Monument

Explore the architectural beauty of the Pakistan Monument, a symbol of national unity.

Visit Daman-E-Koh

Nestled in the lap of the majestic Margalla Hills, Daman-e-Koh is a must-visit tourist spot in Islamabad.
